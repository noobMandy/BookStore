Bhagwad Gita

AC Bahktiveda

The Bhagavad Gita is a sacred Hindu scripture that consists of a conversation between Lord Krishna and the warrior Arjuna on the battlefield of Kurukshetra.

It explores profound philosophical concepts and offers practical guidance on how to live a righteous life. The Gita delves into topics such as duty, righteousness, selfless action, and the nature of the soul. It is revered as a spiritual guide and a source of wisdom for seekers of truth and spiritual enlightenment.

Ramayana

Valmiki

The Ramayana is an ancient Hindu epic that tells the story of Prince Rama and his quest to rescue his wife, Sita, from the demon king Ravana.

It is a tale of love, loyalty, honor, and the triumph of good over evil. The Ramayana portrays the ideals of righteousness, devotion, and dharma (duty) through its characters and their remarkable journey. It has been a significant cultural and religious influence in India and continues to inspire millions with its timeless teachings.

Vishnu Purana

Parashar Muni

The Vishnu Purana is one of the eighteen Mahapuranas of Hindu mythology.

It is a sacred text that primarily focuses on the life, legends, and teachings of Lord Vishnu, the preserver of the universe. The Purana provides detailed accounts of creation, cosmology, genealogy, and various avatars (incarnations) of Lord Vishnu. It offers insights into Hindu cosmology, philosophy, and religious practices, making it a valuable source of knowledge for devotees and scholars alike.

Ramcharit Manas

Tulsidas

"Ramcharitmanas" is an epic poem written by Goswami Tulsidas, depicting the life and exploits of Lord Rama.

It beautifully narrates the story of the divine prince, his journey, and his triumph over evil. Filled with devotion, love, and righteousness, this sacred text explores the ideals of righteousness, devotion, and the path to spiritual enlightenment. Its verses resonate with profound wisdom and inspire millions with their timeless teachings.
